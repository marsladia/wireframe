Engineered for extremes

Nothing else
comes close.

Designed, validated, and built in-house under a former flight-test engineer. Every spec earns its place — and every spec has a plain-language reason to care.

Custom solid-state switch

Rated 1,200A peak / 200A continuous with no moving parts — up to 200× the service life of relay-based alternatives. Why it matters: enough headroom for winches, big-amp audio, and high-CCA builds, with reliability that outlasts mechanical relays.

32-bit ARM adaptive algorithm

A patent-pending dynamic-threshold algorithm learns your vehicle and decides exactly when to isolate. Why it matters: it preserves only the charge you need, so it never shuts off too early or too late.

Essentially invisible drain

Standby draw is about 5mA, dropping below 1 microamp in UltraSleep. Why it matters: the protector itself won't be what drains your battery — even over a full season of storage.

Built to survive anything

Designed to meet or exceed MIL-STD-810H and IP68, with AEC-qualified automotive-grade components and UL94 V-0 flame-resistant materials. Why it matters: dust, mud, vibration, water, and heat to 250°F won't faze it.
