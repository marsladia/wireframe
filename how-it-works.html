How it works

Install once.
Park & WalkAway.™

AlwayStart does the thinking so you don't have to. Here's exactly what happens, from the moment you install it to the moment you turn the key.

Install in minutes — no special tools

AlwayStart sits in the circuit between your battery and the vehicle's electrical loads. Rotate the battery 180°, mount the unit using its integrated bracket, and connect the brass terminals. It's a one-time setup designed so you never touch it again.

It learns your specific vehicle

A 32-bit ARM Cortex processor continuously measures battery energy, health, temperature, and usage patterns. A patent-pending adaptive algorithm calculates the minimum charge needed to start that specific vehicle — a dynamic threshold, not a crude fixed-voltage cutoff.

It isolates the battery automatically

The moment parasitic drain threatens your start reserve, the custom solid-state switch electronically isolates the battery — no cords, no app, no intervention. Because the algorithm is self-calibrating, it isolates only when truly necessary, so you never get a premature shutoff.

You return and press ON

Back at the vehicle, one press of ON reconnects the battery instantly — from the unit itself or the optional wired cabin remote. Turn the key. It starts. Every time.

By stopping parasitic drain at the source, AlwayStart lets a vehicle sit for up to a year with no charging. No cords trailing across the garage. No jump pack that's itself dead. No anxiety the night before a road trip.

3-in-1 monitoring

Always know your battery's status.

Three simple LED indicators tell you everything — protection state, battery health, and charge — at a glance. Add the wired cabin remote to check and reconnect from inside the vehicle.

Protection

Shows whether AlwayStart is actively guarding your start reserve or has isolated the battery.

Health

Surfaces the long-term condition of your battery so you can replace it on your terms, not on the roadside.

Charge

A clear read on remaining charge, so there's never a guessing game before you turn the key.

One honest caveat. AlwayStart is built for gas and diesel vehicles with 12V lead-acid batteries. It is not compatible with EVs, hybrids, Start/Stop vehicles, lithium-battery systems, or 24V configurations. Check your setup before you order — see the full compatibility list.
