The problem

A dead battery is never convenient.

Dead batteries are the #1 reason for roadside calls and the #1 warranty replacement at dealerships. Modern vehicles run 20+ microcontrollers that quietly drain power while parked. A few weeks idle can fully discharge a battery — and every deep discharge accelerates sulfation, permanently shrinking capacity.

Parasitic drain

Alarms, keyless entry, and modules sip current 24/7. Leave the car a few weeks and the starting charge is gone.

Sulfation kills batteries

Each deep discharge builds sulfate crystals that never fully reverse — cutting capacity and lifespan with every cycle.

The old fixes react too late

Trickle chargers and jump packs only help if you remember them — and most respond after the failure, not before.

A new category

Not a charger. Not a jump pack.
A proactive battery protector.

AlwayStart sits between your battery and the vehicle, continuously measuring energy, health, and temperature. It calculates the minimum charge needed to start your specific vehicle — then electronically isolates the battery the moment drain threatens that reserve.

How it works

Install once. Then forget it's there.

Install in minutes

One-time setup, no special tools. Rotate the battery 180°, mount with the integrated bracket, and connect the terminals.

It learns your vehicle

A 32-bit ARM processor runs a patent-pending adaptive algorithm, calculating the exact start reserve your vehicle needs.

It isolates automatically

When parasitic drain threatens that reserve, the solid-state switch silently isolates the battery — preserving your start.

Press ON and go

Back at the vehicle? One press reconnects instantly. Turn the key — it starts. Park & WalkAway.™
